Our Products

BULK SMS

Bulk SMS is a marketing and communication method that involves sending large volumes of SMS messages to a group of recipients simultaneously. Whether you're sending promotions, alerts, reminders, or notifications, our service allows you to send thousands of messages in seconds - all at affordable rates.

BULK VOICE CALL

Bulk Voice is a communication method where pre-recorded voice messages are sent to thousands of recipients simultaneously via phone calls. It’s an effective tool for marketing, alerts, announcements. Messages can be recorded in any language for better localization.

DLT REGISTRATION

DLT Registration is a mandatory process in India for businesses and entities that want to send bulk SMS or voice calls through telecom operators. To ensure that only verified businesses send promotional or transactional messages. To track and monitor SMS/voice content, headers, and sender IDs on a secure platform.

BUTTON WHATSAPP

Button whatsapp messages to send interactive messages with clickable buttons. These enhance engagement and make it easier for users to take action directly from the chat. There are two types of buttons Visit Website - Opens a URL in the browser and Call Phone Number - Initiates a phone call to a specified number.

WHATSAPP API

WhatsApp Business API using Send order updates, OTPs, reminders with Images, PDF and text to make your communication more dynamic. Businesses integrate the API with their backend systems. To send automated, secure, and scalable messages to customers via WhatsApp.

VERIFIED WHATSAPP

Verified Bulk WhatsApp Messaging is Sending messages to multiple users officially via the WhatsApp Business API using a green tick verified business account. Send images, PDFs, videos, location, and CTA buttons. Create interactive messages for better engagement.

BULK MAIL

Bulk mail send to the process of sending a large volume of emails at once to a group of recipients. It is widely used in email marketing, newsletters, announcements, promotions, and notifications. Designed to promote products, services, events, or share important updates.

MISSED CALL ALERTS

Missed Call Alerts is a simple, cost-effective communication method where users give a missed call to a phone number, and the system captures their number and triggers an automated response - such as an SMS, call-back, or campaign enrollment. Trigger automated actions (e.g., send SMS, initiate IVR call, etc.).

IVR

IVR is a technology used in customer support, call centers, and business phone systems that allows a computer to Play recorded voice menus to callers. Let callers respond via keypad (e.g., "Press 1 for Sales"). Route calls, collect information, or trigger actions without human involvement.

Whatsapp marketing

Engage your customers instantly with our Bulk WhatsApp Messaging solution - the fastest and most direct way to reach your audience on the world most popular messaging platform. Send messages with quick-reply and call-to-action buttons to boost engagement and drive instant responses. Share images, videos, documents, and links to make your communication more dynamic.
